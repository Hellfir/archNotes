====== it's just what hArchives ======
Created Monday 18 January 2021

.Zip use p7zip pkg, 
	$ 7z x [file]
	to extract

.rar use unrar pkg,
	$ unrar e [file]
	to extract
	(this works on multi-part files too!)
	
.tar uses
	$tar -xf [file]
